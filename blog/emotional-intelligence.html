Emotional-Intelligence

What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional inteligence is the ability to understand your own feelings and to effectively emphasise with others, to defuse conflict and to communicate clearly. A person with good EQ can recognise, control and express one’s own emotion’s, perceive and assess other’s emotions.

How is it different to IQ?

Iq is a measure of a persons academic intelligence that can measured as a number,which is used to express the intelligence of a person. This can be determined by a persons performance on a standardized intelligence test relative to the average performance of others of the same age.

Why Is it Important

Emotional intelligence is the ability to identify and control your emotions and understand the emotions the others.
